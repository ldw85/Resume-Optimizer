How Resumes Fail the ATS Test

Your application isn't "rejected" by a bot; it's made *invisible* to the recruiter's search. Here are the two main failure points:

1. Parsing Errors

Complex layouts, custom fonts, tables, or graphics can make your resume unreadable to the ATS, resulting in a garbled profile.

2. Keyword Deficiency

If your resume lacks the specific skills and keywords from the job description, you won't appear in the recruiter's search results.
